Admission Procedure

Our admission procedure is designed to ensure transparency and equal opportunity for all applicants. Admissions are granted based on eligibility criteria, interaction/assessment, and seat availability. Parents are encouraged to visit the campus and complete the application process within the specified timeline.

Admission Process

A simple journey to join our community.

01

Visit Campus

Visit personally or virtually to understand facilities and clear your doubts with our admission counsellors.

02

Counselling & Assessment

Professional assessment is conducted to evaluate the student's academic readiness.

03

Application Submission

Submit the completed application form along with required documents and fees.

04

Confirmation Fee Payment

Selected candidates receive confirmation and must pay the fee to secure admission.
