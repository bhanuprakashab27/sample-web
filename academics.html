Academics

Building strong foundations for lifelong learning and success.

Academic Excellence

Sri Vasavi Vidya Samsthe® is committed to delivering strong academic foundations from Pre-KG to Grade 10. With nearly 20 successful SSLC batches, the institution has consistently produced outstanding board results.

The school focuses on disciplined learning, well-qualified teaching staff, and structured academic planning to ensure every student achieves their full potential.

In addition to academics, programs like Spoken English, Expert Handwriting, and Karate training enhance students’ confidence, communication skills, and overall development.

Curriculum Structure

Structured academic journey from foundational learning to SSLC success.

Pre-KG to Montessori

Early foundation focusing on basic literacy, numeracy, discipline, and confidence building in a nurturing environment.

Grades 1 – 7

Strong academic base supported by Expert Handwriting and Spoken English programs to enhance communication and learning skills.

Grades 5 – 7 (Skill Enrichment)

Karate training and co-curricular activities to develop physical fitness, confidence, and self-discipline.

Grades 8 – 10 (SSLC Focus)

Structured academic preparation with disciplined learning and dedicated teaching support, leading to consistent outstanding SSLC board results.
